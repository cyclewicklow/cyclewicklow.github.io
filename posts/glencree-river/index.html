Cycle Wicklow Mountains

Glencree River Valley

1 hour 15 minutes 20 km road cycle route around Glencree River Valley

The area is marked by ruins of the 19th century Ballycorus lead mine flue chimney.
From the top of the hill there are panoramic views of south Dublin and north Wicklow.



Max Elevation 367 meters
Min Elevation 91 meters
Total Elevation Gain 328 meters
