Cycle Wicklow Mountains

Killiney to Bray to Enniskerry

1 hour 45 minutes 30 km road cycle route from Killiney through Bray to Enniskerry

The starting point in Killiney. The small costal road descends to Shankill and then provides comfortable cycle paths all the way through Bray. Better to avoid rush hours due to busy traffic. The road leads to a set of three roundabouts where it goes over N11 and onto Kilcroney Lane. Two right turns take the route onto Church Hill which ascends and descends all the way through Enniskerry. From the village the route continues up the Monastery Road and turns right just before the large Texaco Petrol Station. Left turn goes into a small country road up Barnaslingan Hill. There is an entry to Barnaslingan Wood at the top of the hill. Right turn goes up Carrickgollogan Hill and then around the forest. The road goes down next to Rathmichael Woods. Next right turn leads onto Ballycorus Road which descends all the way to Cherrywood. From there the route goes through Kilbogget Park and back to Killiney.


Max Elevation 217 meters
Min Elevation 4 meters
Total Elevation Gain 518 meters
