Cycle Wicklow Mountains

Cherrywood to Enniskerry

1 hour 15 minutes 23 km road cycle route from Cherrywood through Glencullen to Enniskerry

Starting point at Whelehans Wines car park in Cherrywood near N11.

Right turn at the small roundabout takes the road under M50 overhead pass. The road climbs steadily all the way to Kilternan where there is a small car park next to Adult Education Centre which could serve as an alternate starting point. Next there is long climb towards Glencullen, home of the famous Johnnie Fox’s Pub. The pub has a large overflow car park on the onsite side of the road.

Left turn at the main intersection takes the road downhill pass The Devil’s Elbow which is a particularly steep part of the road which could be hard to pass by car at wintertime. After that the road climbs up again and then descends all the way to the left turn at Kilmolin and then to Enniskerry. From Enniskerry climb up the Monastery Road and turn right just before the large Texaco Petrol Station. Left turn goes into a small country road up the Barnaslingan Hill. There is an entry to Barnaslingan Wood at the top of the hill. Right turn goes up Carrickgollogan Hill and then around the forest. The road goes down next to Rathmichael Woods. Next right turn into Ballycorus Road completes the circle. The road then goes down all the way back to Cherrywood.


Max Elevation 282 meters
Min Elevation 18 meters
Total Elevation Gain 518 meters
