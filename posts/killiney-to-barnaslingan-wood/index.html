Cycle Wicklow Mountains

Killiney to Barnaslingan Wood

1 hour 15 minutes 21 km road cycle route from Killiney to The Barnaslingan Wood

The starting point at The Graduate Roundabout. Best to avoid rush hours as the road towards M50 tends to get busy with traffic. The car park located past N11 at Whelehans Wines shop could be used as an alternate starting point.

The first part of the route is mostly flat. Right turn at the small roundabout leads under M50 overpass and then towards Enniskerry. The road starts to get hilly after the right turn into Ballyman Road. The real challenge awaits after the right turn into a small country road which goes up the Barnaslingan Hill. There is an entry to Barnaslingan Wood at the top of the hill. Right turns goes up Carrickgollogan Hill and around the forest.

The area is marked by ruins of the 19th century Ballycorus lead mine flue chimney. From the top of the hill there are panoramic views of south Dublin and north Wicklow. From this point it is mostly downhill back to the bigger roads and the small roundabout where the route completes the circle.


Max Elevation 218 meters
Min Elevation 16 meters
Total Elevation Gain 367 meters
