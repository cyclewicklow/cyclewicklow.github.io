Cycle Wicklow Mountains

Carrickgollogan Hill

30 minutes 10 km road cycle route around The Carrickgollogan Hill

The hill is marked by ruins of the 19th century Ballycorus lead mine flue chimney.
From the top of the hill there are panoramic views of south Dublin and north Wicklow.





Max Elevation 203 meters
Min Elevation 83 meters
Total Elevation Gain 225 meters
